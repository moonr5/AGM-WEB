About Us

Your Gateway to the Water

PT. Agara Global Maritim (AGM) is a marine services company focused on vessel charter, marine support, and high-quality FRP shipbuilding. With strong operational experience, we serve industrial, tourism, and fishing trip needs with high safety and professionalism standards.

We currently operate charter-ready vessels and continue to expand our fleet through new builds at our shipyard in Marunda, North Jakarta.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading maritime company in Indonesia, providing vessel services, manufacturing, and offshore support with international safety and quality standards.

Mission:

• Provide safe, fast, and reliable vessel charter services.
• Develop the FRP shipbuilding industry with modern technology and best quality.
• Deliver efficient logistics and marine support services.
• Build long-term client relationships through integrity and excellent service.
• Create added value for Indonesia's maritime industry.
